About the Book

"The 108th Line" is a deeply nostalgic, bittersweet coming-of-age novella (e-book) about the crushing expectations of youth, the shared trauma of Indian board exams, and the agonizing beauty of the words we never find the courage to say.

This book is a tribute to the "almosts." It is for anyone who has ever walked out of a school gate, looked back one last time, and carried a "what if" with them for the rest of their life.

About the Author

Roni Mondal is the author of The 108th Line. He specializes in writing deeply emotional, slice-of-life fiction centered around the Indian teenage experience, the weight of academic expectations, and the quiet tragedy of unspoken words. He writes to give a voice to the feelings we usually keep hidden in the margins of our notebooks.
